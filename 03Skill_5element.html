Traditional Chinese Medicine

Traditional Chinese medicine(TCM) pays attention to the "Five Elements, Five Qi, Five Viscera, Five Flavors, and Five Colors". They link, support and restrain each other. Whether the balance of the Five Elements is directly related to the operation of the body. If one of the Five Elements is strong or weak, corresponding physical symptoms will appear. In TCM terms, your body in disharmony. Therefore, understanding the Five Elements of the body is also necessary for health.

  The Five Elements could generate each other, whereas they restrict each other. According to the relationship of generation that would be Wood produces Fire; Fire creates Earth(soil); Earth(soil) bears Metal; Metal carries Water; and Wood are nourished by Water. Whereas, according to the relationship of restrain, that would be Wood compress(restrict) Soil; Soil absorbs Water ; Water quenches Fire; Fire melts Metal; and Metal chops Wood.
