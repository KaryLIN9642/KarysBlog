求其放心而已(seek for the lost mind)

孟子曰：「仁，人心也；義，人路也。舍其路而弗由，放其心而不知求，哀哉！人有雞犬放，則知求之；有放心而不知求。學問之道無他，求其放心而已矣。」


Benevolence is man's mind, and righteousness is man's path. How lamentable is it toneglect the path and not pursue it, to lose this mind and not know to seek it again! When men's fowls and dogs are lost, they know to seek for them again, but they lose their mind, and do not know to seek for it. The great end of learning is nothing else but to seek for the lost mind.

Clarissa Austin

On 1/3/2023, an excellent, professional, straightforward, keep her promise, kind and respected lady left us, this is definitely a sad day.
  I still remember the scene when Clarissa was invited to come to Taiwan for the first time to give a lecture and visit our company at the end of August 2008. At that time, I used my poor English to ask her so many questions about Testing Standards. She explained my questions kindly and told me that if I have further questions, don't hesitate to email her. After that, no matter how busy she is, she will always respond to my questions. She is definitely my mentor and role model in the area of geosynthetics.
  I hope to use this way to honour the memory of Clarissa Austin.
  May she rest in peace.

Dr. Jane Goodall

That was a small meet-and-greet on a beautiful afternoon in November 2000 on the NPUST campus. It was also the first time I saw her very closely, an elegant, kindly lady, Dr. Jane Goodall.
  Dr. Jane Goodall, who is an English primatologist, anthropologist also renowned animal conservationist and environmental advocate. She has received many honours for her environmental, humanitarian and others work. In 2019, Time magazine named Goodall as one of the 100 most influential people in the world.
  Dr Jane Goodall said: “Everything is connected — everyone can make a difference. If each one of us reduces the consumption of water or meat in ore diet; or electricity, we are millions of people reducing the consumption of resources. Let's use the gift of our lives to make this a batter world for all.”
