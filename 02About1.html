壽豐鄉鹽寮村原民聚落後方擋土牆新建工程

Construction project of the retaining wall behind the aboriginal settlement of Yanliao Village

Project Brief:

New constructed retaining walls will be located behind the aboriginal settlement of Yanliao village. The aboriginal settlement was next to the coastal mountains, however when heavy rain occurs, rain impacts the ground surface, and causes the foundation was damaged over time, which in turn affects the stability of the slopes. These new retaining walls were built to improve the living safety for residents.
  There are 2 gravity retaining walls in total, the first one is 30m long and the second one is 75m long.
 1. For first one retaining wall, the first 20m of wall height is 3m, and the foundation is 1.2m deep into the soil layer, and the next 10m, the wall height is 5.2m, and the foundation 1.2m deep.
 2. The second retaining wall, the first 50m of wall height is 4.7m, and the foundation is 2.5m deep, and the next 25m, the wall height is 3.7m, and the foundation 1.2m deep.

Project info:

  • Date - 2021/07 ~ 2021/12
  • Tender Awarding Price - about 120,000 USD
  • construction period - about 5 months
